Hope A. Frost

  • Nickname: Frosty
  • Age: 14
  • Birthday: October 31st
  • Gender: Female
  • Appearance: Short, messy blond hair. Orange eyes. 5'3". Tough build.
  • Key Personality Traits: Hot-headed, Protective, Vindictive
  • Greatest Desire: Help others
  • Worst Fear: Having harm come to her loved ones
    •

      Hope sees the world as a breeding ground for darkness and evil… and she is no exception to this rule. Being the child of a super-hot mom and being interested in dark topics like death, murder, creepy-crawlies, and violence make her a target for bullying, but that doesn't bother her… much. What does bother her is the fact that these evil-doers can't seem to keep their hands off of other people…

      How she deals with these crooks, monsters, and evil-doers is the only correct way--violence. She's knocked out teeth, broken bones, and spent several nights in juvenile detention for it. That doesn't stop her pursuit of “justice,” however.

      Underneath all this bravado, however, she is just a kid… a kid seeking love and acceptance. Aww….. so cute….

      Despite not being the most creative, she can pick up on the little details and half-piece them together to come to a thrilling conclusion that can lead to more punches. Plus, her memory is basically unstoppable.

    Relationships

    VVOYD

    Thinks of him as another thing to punch. Is constantly in a battle of control with him, thinks his ideals of chaos and his “artistic endeavors” are dangerous with no practical use.

    HARMONY

    Naturally, she thinks of Harmony as the good, smart older twin who makes her feel bad about herself. Though, because they've spent so much time together, and because Harmony seems to be the only one her age not completely terrified of her, she has a soft spot for her “older” sister.

    PHILLIP

    Though she originally blamed Phillip for marrying such an ungodly monster, she's recently become more suspicious of him. However, he's giving her more attention and love than ever before-the one thing she's been craving-so her feelings towards him are a little complicated.

    AURORA

    She KNOWS her mom doesn't like her, and that's fine, because Hope doesn't like Aurora either. She wants proof that Aurora is as comically villainous as the other people she fights so she has a solid reason to knock her teeth in, but she keeps her anger to herself when she's around her dad.

    AXOLOTL

    She can appreciate his want for a normal life, and rather admires that he can live so freely. He exudes darkness, yet holds friends close to him, and he's shown her kindness in quiet moments… She's got a soft spot for him, too.
