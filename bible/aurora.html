Aurora Ashbluff-Frost

  • Name/Nickname: Aurora Ashbluff-Frost
  • Age: 33
  • Birthday: December 8th
  • Gender: Female
  • Appearance: Supermodel slender figure, beautiful brunette hair, looks like she's always smelling something awful.
  • Key Personality Traits: Courageous, Vain, One-Sided
  • Greatest Desire: The normal... money, riches, fame.
  • Worst Fear: Being abandoned
    •

      The finer things in life are what make the effort of breathing worth-while. She uses her beauty to garner fame and riches for herself, filling a void in her heart left by the lack of parental love and attention in her childhood. Though she may seem superficial on the surface, somewhere, deep, deep, deep, deep inside of her diamond-edged heart she cares for other people. She just doesn't know how to show it.
