Vvoyd

  • Name/Nickname: Vvoyd
  • Age: ? really, really old, lol.
  • Birthday: 14/40
  • Gender: Male
  • Appearance: Doesn't ever stay the same... likes orange, though.
  • Greatest Desire: Overthrow the government (not ours, though)
  • Worst Fear: Being trapped in Hope FOREVER
    •

      Vvoyd has always found humans fascinating, if a little terrifying. Watching them from a distance? Fine. But being fused to one? Not in his plans. An artist by nature, a spy by trade and a traitor by legal definition, Vvoyd longs for freedom from this… ugh… bipedal lifeform.

      However, the world is ever-changing and Vvoyd be damned if he's caught not changing with it. However, this new body is so resistant to his expressive shape-shifting, which he has a constant need to do to avoid artistic insanity—and a cramp. Though ancient by our standards, he's just hitting his mid-life crisis. The Lost Dimension is a realm of eternal beings, where few are as… free-thinking and creative as Vvoyd. His ties to that world are anything but friendly, but he would love to go back and finish what he started—without being trapped inside a moody teenager. Also, his memory isn't the best.
